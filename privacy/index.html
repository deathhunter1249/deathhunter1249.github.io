What We Collect

When you verify through our system, we collect and store your Discord user ID, your Roblox user ID, your Roblox username, and the date and time of verification. We do not collect passwords, email addresses, or any other personal information.

How We Use It

The data we collect is used solely to link your Roblox account to your account within the DPRT community server. This allows us to verify your group membership and assign appropriate roles. We do not use your data for advertising, analytics, or any other purpose.

Data Storage

Your data is stored on a private server and is not shared with any third parties. We do not sell, trade, or transfer your information to outside parties under any circumstances.

Data Removal

You may request removal of your data at any time by contacting a staff member in the DPRT community server. Upon request, your verification record will be permanently deleted.

Third Party Services

Our verification system uses the Roblox OAuth 2.0 API. By verifying, you also agree to the Roblox Terms of Use.
